About Plasma

A real-time interactive 3D simulation combining high-performance computer vision and WebGL rendering.

  • Hand tracking: Captures joint coordinates via WebRTC camera streams and MediaPipe Hands ML.
  • Tension Mapping: Joint distance vectors are normalized against palm width to extract a gesture ratio.
  • Morphing: Points interpolate between mathematical target formulas with organic noise.
